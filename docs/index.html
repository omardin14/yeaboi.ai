01 / analysis

Know how your team actually delivers.

Reads your Jira or Azure DevOps history ‚ÄĒ velocity, estimation accuracy, churn ‚ÄĒ and builds a team profile that calibrates every plan yeaboi makes after it.

02 / planning

Description in, sprint plan out.

A five-stage pipeline ‚ÄĒ analysis, features, stories, tasks, sprints ‚ÄĒ with a review checkpoint after every stage. Accept, edit, or reject; then push to your board.

03 / standup

The daily that runs itself.

Detects what everyone shipped from commits and tickets, scores sprint-day confidence, and delivers the summary to terminal, Slack, or email ‚ÄĒ on an OS schedule, even with yeaboi closed.

04 / retro

A board the whole team joins.

Spins up a live board any browser on the network can join ‚ÄĒ cards, reactions, a shared timer. When the talking's done, AI drafts the action items.

05 / performance

1:1s backed by real work.

Preps each 1:1 from the engineer's actual delivery and last meeting's open actions, writes up the summary after, and synthesizes six-month reviews when they're due.

06 / reporting

Tell the business what shipped.

Turns a sprint, a month, or a whole quarter of delivered work into an executive summary ‚ÄĒ as a slide deck, HTML, or Markdown.

07 / usage

Costs, in the open.

Token usage, session history, and cost estimates for every LLM call ‚ÄĒ tracked per session, right in the app.

08 / settings

Configured without leaving the terminal.

API keys, LLM provider, board connections, log level, data directory ‚ÄĒ all managed in-app, validated as you go.

description in ‚ÄĒ sprint plan out

Five stages, you stay in charge.

Planning runs a five-stage pipeline with a review checkpoint after every stage. Nothing lands in your backlog without your sign-off.

Analyze

Your description and intake answers become a structured project analysis ‚ÄĒ goals, risks, constraints.

Features

The analysis decomposes into prioritised features, Critical through Low.

Stories

Persona/goal/benefit stories with Given-When-Then acceptance criteria and Fibonacci points.

Tasks

Concrete tasks with labels, test plans, and ready-to-paste AI coding prompts.

Sprints

Stories allocated against net velocity ‚ÄĒ holidays, PTO, and onboarding deducted.

human-in-the-loopAt every checkpoint: [1] Accept [2] Edit [3] Reject ‚ÄĒ edit inline or regenerate with feedback before anything moves on.
